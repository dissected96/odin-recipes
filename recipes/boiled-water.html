BOILED WATER

Welcome to the second easiest recipe ever!

Ingredients

  • A pot
  • Some water

Instructions

  1. First of all, you need to find a pot. Be careful, these are not easy to find nowadays.
  2. Just after finding the pot, pour some water in it, yeah. I know it is not as easy as it sounds.
  3. Put the pot on the stove, activate the stove aaaaaaaand voila! You got yourself a freshly boiled water!
